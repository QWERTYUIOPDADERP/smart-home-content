The Convenience of the Smart Home™

The Smart Home™ is designed for convenience, a contains top of the line technology including, but not limited to:

Smart Clock


This clock will read the date and time to you out loud for you wherever in the house you are located.

Voice Assistant


The voice assistant is designed to cater to your schedule, capable of settings reminders for you and planning your day.

Automated Stove


The automated stove makes breakfast... on its own! Avoid the challenge of cooking for the family with the self-cooking stove!

Automated Doors


All the doors in the house, including the garage door, will learn your schedule and open when needed, wihtout you doing anything.

Bed Warmers


Ever feel cold at night, unable to get warm under the covers? Well these beds warm themselves!

Baths


The Smart Bathing technology integrated into the Smart Home™ automatically starts the bath for you at the prefect temperature.

Cleaning


The house is designed so that you will never have to clean again!
Metal mice clean up everything, and that means EVERYTHING!
Dust, dirt, mud, leaves, rotting flesh, and everything in-between.
