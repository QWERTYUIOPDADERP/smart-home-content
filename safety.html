The Safety of the Smart Home™

Our safety amenitites designed to ensure the safety of you, your family, and your belongings include:

Fire Alarm


In the unlikely event of a fire, our advanced voice alerts and warning lights spring into action, ensuring you're immediately aware of any danger.
With crystal-clear vocal notifications and bright, attention-grabbing lights, you'll have peace of mind knowing that safety is always a top priority.

Fire sprinkler


Our state-of-the-art system features strategically placed water jets in the walls and ceiling that activate at the first sign of fire.
This innovative approach ensures rapid and effective suppression, helping to control and extinguish flames swiftly.

Automated fire extinguishing hoses


Our revolutionary system features over 20 automated hoses, each equipped to spray our patented anti-fire green chemicals.
Designed for maximum effectiveness, these advanced hoses activate at the first sign of a fire, delivering a powerful, eco-friendly solution that quickly extinguishes flames.

Criter detection and warding


Our smart system not only lets your pets in with ease but also keeps pesky mice and birds at bay.
With advanced sensors and automated controls, your furry friends enjoy seamless access, while unwanted critters are gently repelled.

Password protection


The home's security is enhanced with cutting-edge voice password protection!
Only those you trust can gain access, thanks to a sophisticated voice recognition system that ensures precise and secure entry.
With this advanced technology, you control who enters your home, providing peace of mind and an extra layer of safety.
